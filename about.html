About

Hello I'm Lukas. I built Musician Key early 2017. It was one of the first web projects I built in order to learn the basics of web development. It looked much different than it does today, and it will probably look much different tomorrow. I am constantly trying out new things the more I learn and will be changing things frequently in order to find the most convenient ways to help musicians learn, teach, and use music theory.

I will be adding more and more content in the coming year so please check back often!
